Restaurant About Us

About Us

Welcome to Jolly Savory, where the flavors of India come to life! I'm Swetha, and my love for authentic Indian cuisine inspired the creation of this culinary haven. At Jolly Savory, we are passionate about delivering the rich and diverse tastes of India to your plate. Our dedicated team, led by our skilled chef, works tirelessly to ensure every dish is a masterpiece of flavors and aromas. We take pride in using the finest, locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that transport you to the heart of India. Our goal is to offer you not just a meal but an experience, where the warmth of Indian culture meets your senses. Whether you're a long-time fan of Indian cuisine or new to its delights, we invite you to savor the magic of Jolly Savory and embark on a gastronomic journey like no other. Come join us, and let's celebrate the joy of Indian flavors together!

Restaurant Menu

Sample Menu

Mutton Biriyani

Mutton Biriyani is a flavorful South Asian dish made with tender pieces of mutton.

This traditional dish is known for its rich and savory taste, making it a popular choice for those who enjoy a hearty and delicious meal.

Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal.

It is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a satisfying breakfast,snack,or even a meal,loved for its delighted taste and wide range of fillings and toppings.

Chicken Biriyani

Chicken biriyani is a classic South Asian dish that combains succulent pieces of chicken with aromatic spices.

This iconic dish is a feast for the senses, embodying the rich heritage of South Asian Cuisine.

Butter Nann

Butter Nann is a soft and fluffy Indian flatbread that's loved for its melt-in-the-mouth goodness.

Each piece is generously brushed with creamy,aromatic butter,creating a hevenly combination of flavors and textures.

Full Meals

Elevate your dining experience with our sumptuous Full Meals.

Whether you're a fan of itaditional cuisines or international flavours,our Full Meals are a tantalizing journey for your palate,leaving you fully satiated and delighted.
